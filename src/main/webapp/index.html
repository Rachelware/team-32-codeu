Welcome to Virtual Escape, an online Escape Room experience!

General Instructions:

Click the links above to login and visit your page.

On your page, you will be presented with a puzzle.

Starting on level 1, each puzzle is accompanied by a chat room full of others attempting to solve that same puzzle!

You can post messages and help each other out!

Once you solve the puzzle, you are immediately pushed into the next level and entered into a new chat room filled with new people also on your level.

Each level gets successively harder, and once you reach the end - you have successfully ESCAPED!

Good luck!
