The following clues will lead you on a global scavenger hunt. The answer to each clue is a city somewhere in the world. To submit your response, drop a map pin on each city! In order to answer the puzzle correctly, all three pins must be placed within 1 degree of latitude and longitude of the city we are looking for. Good Luck!

CLUE #1

A sparkle in water. Hope for the poor.
Suddently, I was a fronteir no more!
Franticly, they flocked to my state,
for rare treasure discovered in 1848.
Drop a pin on my capitol city!

CLUE #2

Uranium, graphite, dosimeters, oh my!
A ghost-town, wasteland, accident, am I.
Who's fault, you ask? Don't say a name!
My state played a cruel, sick game.
Drop a pin on my city!

CLUE #3

Vamos a El Centro! Old walls, so high,
seat us as we see lively boats drive by!
Don't burn, stay cool, dance, and enjoy
My color, my vibrance, my history ...
Oh and a cute mochila! Wow! Me voy!
Drop a pin on my city!
